+
+```gradle
+dependencies {
+  compile 'com.squareup.dagger:dagger:${dagger.version}'
+  provided 'com.squareup.dagger:dagger-compiler:${dagger.version}'
+}
+```

 [Dagger][dl-dagger], [JavaWriter][dl-javawriter], and [javax.inject][dl-inject].
